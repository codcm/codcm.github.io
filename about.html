About Us

Our Mission

Code Com is a platform that teaches people how to develop the web. It enables people to be effective as much as possible through teaching them coding with several exercises and projects. Our Mission is to make the web development accessible to anyone who wants to know about it.

Our Story

The developers of Code Com are two web developers, Solen Abebe and Kena Abebe, who had the passion to teach what they know to the community. They started their web development journey in the past few years. After aquiring the knowledge of web development, they decided to share the stunning world of Web development. Finally they decided upon teaching the web through the knowledge they gained. They used their knowledge to give knowledge.
